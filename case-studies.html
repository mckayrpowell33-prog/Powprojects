Case studies

Example outcomes from real-world workflows

These anonymized scenarios show typical outcomes and target metrics from AI Operations projects.

How to read these

Each example is based on common projects. Results are presented as typical outcomes or example targets, not guarantees.

Real estate lead follow-up automation

Problem: Leads were coming in from multiple sources and follow-up was inconsistent.

Solution: Automated intake and routing to the right agent, with templated replies and CRM updates.

Typical outcomes / example targets: Faster response times within minutes, fewer missed leads, and clear activity reporting.

Small business inbox + scheduling assistant

Problem: A growing service business struggled to keep up with inquiries and appointment scheduling.

Solution: AI-assisted inbox triage, scheduling workflows, and confirmations synced to a calendar tool.

Typical outcomes / example targets: Lower admin hours each week, improved booking accuracy, and consistent client communication.

Spreadsheet reporting + weekly summary automation

Problem: Weekly reporting required manual data cleanup and hours of work.

Solution: Automated data pulls, standardized templates, and AI summaries for leadership review.

Typical outcomes / example targets: Reporting completed in under an hour, fewer errors, and faster decisions.
